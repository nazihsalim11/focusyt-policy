Privacy Policy — FocusYT

Last updated: 29/04/2026

FocusYT is a Chrome extension that hides distracting elements on YouTube. We take privacy seriously because the extension does almost nothing that requires data.

What we do not collect

  • We do not collect your YouTube history, watch behaviour, or any browsing data.
  • We do not collect your name, email, IP address, or any account information.
  • We do not use analytics, telemetry, or tracking pixels.
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties.

What is stored locally on your device

Your toggle preferences (which features are on or off) and, if you have purchased Pro, your license key. These are stored in chrome.storage.sync — Chrome's built-in storage that may sync across your signed-in Chrome browsers if you have Chrome sync enabled.

The only network request

If you enter a Pro license key, the extension sends that key to api.lemonsqueezy.com (our payment provider) to confirm the key is valid. This happens once when you activate, and once every 24 hours afterwards to detect cancellations or refunds. No other data is sent.

Payments

Pro purchases are processed by Lemon Squeezy. Their privacy policy applies to that transaction: https://www.lemonsqueezy.com/privacy.

Children

FocusYT is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any data from any user.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new "last updated" date.
